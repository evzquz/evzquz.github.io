About EvX

EvxDevelop is a freelance business for your web development, photography, and videography needs. My name is Erika and I started EvxDevelop in December 2019. I currently reside in Houston, Texas. I hold a degree in Computer Science, and have some work experience as a Full-stack Web Developer. I also have a lot of experience in customer service positions, and enjoy providing quality service to all clients. I have always had a huge interest in digital media, particularly working with computers. Programming, graphic design, video editing, etc.

I film, edit and produce movies and short videos in HD and even 4k! I’m also able to shoot some awesome profesional-looking photos! My greatest specialty though, is building websites and web applications. Some of the technologies I work with include: HTML/CSS, JavaScript, Java, Node, C++, SQL for databases, ReactJs framework, Git, and am comfortable in Windows and Linux (Ubuntu, Arch, Mint) OS environments.

On my free time I like cycling, nature walks, reading, piano and guitar playing, programming (soo fun I do it in my free time), and attending local rock shows. Some of my aspirations include: owning a big sucessful business, helping build better schools in poverty-ridden places, and owning a personal slurpee machine.

I am a very down to earth and friendly individual. My love for learning is infinite, and I am always looking for new opportunities and connections. If you are looking to get your own custom made website, look no further, I offer awesome skills and affordable prices. If you are looking for a photographer or videographer for your next wedding, party, or event (and are willing to invite me), book EvXDevelop now and let’s connect!
